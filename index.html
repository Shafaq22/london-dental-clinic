About Us

A Legacy of Dental Excellence

Serving Mumbai since 1993 with compassion, precision & cutting-edge technology

London Dental Clinic, led by Dr. Shruti Agarwal Garg, has been a cornerstone of dental healthcare in Kandivali East, Mumbai for over three decades. Our commitment is simple: deliver world-class dental care with warmth and precision.

From routine check-ups to advanced laser treatments, we offer a comprehensive spectrum of dental services for patients of all ages. Every treatment plan is customized to your unique needs, ensuring you leave with not just a healthier smile — but a confident one.
